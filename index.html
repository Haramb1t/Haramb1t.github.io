Om Flemming About Flemming

Flemming Pedersen blev født den 25. juni 1965 i Sæby i Nordjylland tæt ved havet.

Et sted, hvor blikket naturligt søger horisonten, og hvor man tidligt fornemmer, at verden er større end det, der ligger lige foran ens øjne.

Han voksede op i Salbrovad, hvor han lærte værdien af hårdt arbejde, af at arbejde målrettet og af at skabe noget med sine egne hænder. Dette blev et fundament, som han bar med sig gennem hele livet.

Dette blev et fundament, som han bar med sig gennem hele livet. Hans vej ind i skibsmæglerbranchen begyndte, da han kom i lære hos firmaet Fage Pedersen. Her fik han indsigt i branchen og fandt et arbejde, der passede til hans temperament og evner. Senere overtog han virksomheden, videreførte driften og blev selvstændig.

En stor del af hans arbejdsliv kredsede om shipping og international handel. Han arbejdede med fragt, entreprenører og forbindelser på tværs af landegrænser - og han beskæftigede sig også med køb og salg af blandt andet træflis og sukkerroer. Gennem sine virksomheder Fageship ApS og A. Fage Pedersens Eftf. arrangerede han fragtforsendelser af træpiller, tømmerstokke, salt, kul og brænde til havne langs de danske og europæiske kyster.

Det var en verden, hvor timing, overblik og forhandlingsevner var afgørende, og hvor Flemming følte sig hjemme.

Han havde et skarpt blik for muligheder og modet til at tage chancer, når han troede på en idé.

Han var velformuleret, skabte let kontakt og kunne begå sig ubesværet i mange forskellige sammenhænge.

For ham var samtalen ikke blot en udveksling af ord, men en måde at opbygge relationer på. Han kunne være direkte, men også charmerende, og han satte pris på den gode dialog.

Han rejste meget. Lufthavne, havne og hoteller blev en del af hans hverdag. Men uanset hvor i verden han befandt sig, bar han altid sit udgangspunkt med sig - og havde altid øje for en god handel.

Flemming gik også op i sit udseende. Han værdsatte kvalitet, stil og et godt udtryk. Han kørte Jaguar, klædte sig pænt, havde sans for detaljer - og nød at omgive sig med ting, der signalerede smag og personlighed.

Men et menneske er mere end sit arbejde - og mere end sit ydre. Flemming var et menneske med drivkraft, beslutsomhed og engagement i alt, hvad han foretog sig.

Den 28. januar 2026 sov Flemming stille ind i Assens efter en tids sygdom. Han efterlader sig tre børn: Alexander, Michelle og Patrick, samt en kreds af venner, kolleger og forretningsforbindelser, der mindes ham som en pålidelig, dedikeret og varm person.

Tilbage er minderne. Mange forskellige minder. Minder, som bæres videre af dem, der kendte ham, gennem de mennesker, han mødte på sin vej gennem livet.

Flemming Pedersen was born on 25 June 1965 in Sæby in North Jutland, close to the sea.

A place where one's gaze naturally seeks the horizon, and where one early senses that the world is larger than what lies immediately before one's eyes.

He grew up in Salbrovad, roughly 6,5 km from Assens, where he learned the value of hard work, of working with purpose, and of creating something with his own hands. This became a foundation he carried with him throughout his life.

His path into the shipping brokerage business began when he entered an apprenticeship at the firm Fage Pedersen. Here he gained insight into the industry and found work that suited his temperament and abilities. He later took over the company, continued its operation, and became self employed.

Much of his working life revolved around shipping and international trade. He worked with freight, contractors, and connections across national borders--and he was also involved in the buying and selling of, among other things, wood chips and sugar beets. Through his companies Fageship ApS and A. Fage Pedersens Eftf., he arranged cargo shipments of wood pellets, timber logs, salt, coal and firewood to ports along Danish and European coastlines.

It was a world where timing, overview, and negotiating skill were crucial, and where Flemming felt at home.

He had a sharp eye for opportunity and the courage to take chances when he believed in an idea.

He was articulate, made contact easily, and was able to move comfortably in many different settings.

For him, conversation was not merely an exchange of words, but a way of building relationships. He could be direct, yet also charming, and he valued good dialogue.

He traveled a great deal. Airports, ports, and hotels became part of his everyday life. But no matter where in the world he was, he carried his point of depature with him--always with an eye for a deal.

Flemming also cared about his appearance. He appreciated quality, style, and a good look. He drove a Jaguar, dressed well, had an eye for detail--and enjoyed surrounding himself with things that signaled taste and personality.

But a human being is more than his work--and more than his outward appearance. Flemming was a person with drive, determination, and commitment to whatever he undertook.

On the 28. january 2026, Flemming passed away peacefully in Assens after a long period of illness. He is survived by three children: Alexander, Michelle and Patrick - as well as a circle of friends, colleagues and business associates who remember him as a reliable, dedicated and warm-hearted man.

What remains are the memories. Many different memories. Memories carried by those who knew him through the people he met along the way in life.
